Morning desert safari

Written by M Zakarya April 5, 2021
120 aed

✪ Pick up from anywhere Dubai, Sharjah in Toyota 4×4 Land Cruiser.
✪ Capture your moments with Beautiful Sunset and sunrise Scene.
✪ Camel Riding.
✪ sand boarding.
✪ 30 Minutes adventurous Dune Bashing with professional tour guide.
✪ Desert photography.
✪ Soft drinks and cold water.
✪ Quad BIke option availible(Extra charges).
✪ Drive back to city and drop off your location.

✪TOUR Details✪

We do pick up services from your doorstep at most locations within Sharjha & Dubai. We begin with a commute to the paid quad bike ride location for a firsthand experience of driving in the desert. 4×4 vehicles then enter the desert as a convoy for the most exciting dune bashing session. Photo opportunities with the endless desert in the background are a part of this excursion. Sand Boarding, Camel Riding, Delicious Cocktails, Live Barbeque and much more await you at the camp. The Desert Safari expert would then await to take you back to the pickup location.

✪TOUR Shedule✪

✪ Pick up Time: 8.00am - 9:00am.
✪ Pick up Time for afternoon: 2.30am - 3:30am.
✪ Duration: 4:00 Hrs
